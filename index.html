frequently asked questions

How cancel hotel reservation?

We understand that sometimes plans change and you may need to cancel or modify your reservation. We kindly ask that you inform us of any changes as soon as possible.
The following cancellation policy applies to all reservations made directly with our hotel:
You can cancel your reservation for free up to 48 hours before your arrival date. We will refund your deposit in full, minus a $25 administration fee.

What are payment methods?

We accept the following payment methods for your convenience:
Credit cards: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover cards. Cash: You can pay with cash at the hotel when you check-in or check-out.
PayPal: You can pay online with your PayPal account when you make your reservation.
You will be redirected to the PayPal website to complete your payment.

How can I make a reservation for another person?

You can make a reservation for another person by entering their name and contact details in the guest information section of the booking process. You will also need to inform the hotel of the name of the person who will be staying and present a copy of their identification at check-in.

How do I know if my hotel reservation has been confirmed successfully?

You will receive an email confirmation with your reservation details and a booking reference number after you complete your reservation. You can also check your reservation status online by logging into your account or entering your booking reference number and email address on our website.

What do I do if I don’t receive the email confirmation of my hotel reservation?

If you don’t receive the email confirmation of your hotel reservation within 24 hours, please check your spam or junk folder. If you still can’t find it, please contact us by phone or email and we will resend it to you.
